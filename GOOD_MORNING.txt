THE ROOT CAUSE:

POSSIBLE CAUSES:
1. Model input preprocessing issue (image scaling)
2. Wrong BlazeFace variant (PINTO vs MediaPipe vs custom)
3. Missing anchor-based decoding (model outputs deltas, not absolutes)
4. Model trained on different resolution
